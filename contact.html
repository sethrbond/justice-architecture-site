For Potential Partner Organizations

If you're a 501(c)(3) providing legal defense, asylum representation, or immigration services in one of our eight priority states and you're interested in a Discovery Protocol partnership, reach out using the form. There is no formal application — it starts with a conversation.

For Funders & Donors

Contributions to The Justice Architecture Fund are tax-deductible under Section 501(c)(3). We issue IRS-compliant donor acknowledgement tax receipts for all contributions. Use the form or email us directly to discuss how your support reaches the frontline.

For Experts & Volunteers

We're building our network of technologists, researchers, and policy advocates for when we transition to the open-source tool-building phase. Tell us about your expertise and how you'd like to contribute.

Response Time
We respond to all inquiries within 3 business days. Partner organization inquiries and funding conversations are prioritized.

Your information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not share contact information with third parties.

The Justice Architecture Fund is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization registered in Washington State. Contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Justice Architecture Action is a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization. Contributions are not tax-deductible as charitable donations.

Both entities operate under a formal Resource Sharing Agreement and maintain separate governance, bank accounts, and financial records.
