Privacy Policy

How we handle your information. Last updated March 2026.

What we collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect your name, email address, organization name (if provided), and your message. That's it.

How it's processed

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. Your submission is sent directly to our team via Formspree's servers.

How we use it

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry. If you reach out about a partnership, a contribution, or a general question, we use your contact details to reply, and that's all.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with third parties for marketing. We don't add you to mailing lists. We don't use your information for any purpose other than responding to your message.

Cookies and tracking

This site may use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous, aggregate data about how visitors use the site (pages visited, session duration, general location). When enabled, this helps us understand how people find us and what content is useful. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Third-party services

This site uses three third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4, for anonymous traffic analytics. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts, for typography. Google may collect basic connection data (IP address) when fonts are loaded. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Formspree, for contact form handling. See Formspree's privacy policy.

Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. We don't maintain a long-term database of form submissions.

Your rights

If you'd like to request deletion of any data you've submitted through our contact form, email us at info@justicearchitecture.org and we'll take care of it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
